They said that students should learn along with their curiosity, so forcible homework should be abolished. However, elementary school students’ curiosity is limited to narrow range because they do not experience various things. The training through homework can cultivate students’ perspectives, values, and curiosities. If curiosity-based learning is only implemented, their curiosity is intensive, but their perspectives are not expanded. However, homework makes students experience various thing. The characteristic of elementary schools that give shallow and width information is applicable for the latter studying style. Therefore, primary school should not depend on children limited curiosities.
